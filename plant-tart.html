Man-Eating Plant Tart

A mixture of mashed up and cut up Man-Eating Plant fruit, slime and scorpion soup is poured into a pan lined with the flattened peel of the fruit and cooked before garnishing with some more fruit.

Ingredients

(serves 3-4)

  • Leftover scorpion broth (you can use another broth if you want) (200 ml)
  • Slime gelatin (80 grams)
  • Baraselia (3 med. size)
  • Meeroak (5 or 6 med. size)
  • Betan (3 med. size)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (a little)

Directions

  1. First, steam the fruits lightly, cut a circle under the stalk twist a bit and pull it out.
  2. Hit the peeled skin from the fruit to soften it up, then line a frying pan with it.
  3. Mash up the unripe fruit, and add some slime and scorpion soup
  4. Stir until it thickens it up and it's smooth.
  5. Add the rest of the scorpion soup and some roughly chopped fruit and mix it a bit.
  6. Put it into the frying pan and let it heat up.
  7. Once the surface begins tu bubble garnish it with the last of the fruit.
  8. Finished!
