Huge Scorpion and Walking Mushroom Hotpot

Scorpion, mushroom, algae, tubers and dried slime noodles are all boiled together in water, creating a light soup.

Ingredients

(serves 3-4)

  • Huge Scorpion (1)
  • Walking Mushroom (1)
  • Mushroom feet (2)
  • Seaweed (artic moss, star jelly) (as needed)
  • Invertatoes (about 5 med. size)
  • Dried slime (to preference)
  • Water (as needed)

Directions

  1. Cut off the pincers, head, legs and tail of the Huge Scorpion.
  2. Cut scores along the body of the Huge Scorpion and take out the innards.
  3. Throw away the rump and three centimeters of the surface of the walking Mushroom (but keep the legs).
  4. Cut the seawwd and the invertatoes and add it to the pan.
  5. Cut the dried slime in thin stripes and add it to the pan.
  6. Cook it until the Huge Scorpion's meat aquires a golden color.
  7. Finished!
